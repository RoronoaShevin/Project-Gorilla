About Gorilla

Built like the
animal we're named after.

A silverback doesn't rely on speed or aggression. It relies on consistency, a plant-based diet, and a troop that looks out for each other. That's the model behind every brand we run.

Our story

From one gym to a troop of brands.

Gorilla started as a single training floor with a simple rule: no fads, no shortcuts, just consistent work and real food. As members kept asking the same two questions — what should I eat, and where can I buy it — the company grew into the three brands it runs today.

Year one

Ironback opens its first floor

A single multipurpose gym, built around strength training, conditioning, and recovery in one space.

Year three

Canopy Nutrition launches

Members kept asking what to eat. Canopy started as in-house coaching and grew into a standalone consultancy.

Year five

Wildfuel goes to market

Canopy's recipes became products. Wildfuel now supplies gyms, retailers, and Ironback locations directly.

Today

Three brands, one troop

Gorilla Group now oversees all three, sharing standards, coaching, and formulation across the business.
